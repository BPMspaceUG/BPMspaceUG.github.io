AI on Boat (Open Source)

Build a robust, privacy-first onboard data and automation platform with open-source components — and add AI where it truly helps.

What's included (MVP)

  • Data bus & integrations: NMEA 2000/0183 via CAN + Signal K; MQTT messaging
  • Apps & dashboards: OpenCPN (nav), Grafana + InfluxDB (telemetry), Node-RED (automations), optional Home Assistant
  • AI touchpoints: log triage, checklists, anomaly detection (on-device or edge)
  • Hardware profile: Raspberry Pi 4/5, CAN/N2K interface, LTE/WiFi router, GNSS, IMU
  • Remote access: WireGuard or Tailscale (optional)

Why this approach?

  • No vendor lock-in — standard protocols (Signal K / MQTT)
  • Offline-first — full control on board
  • Maintainable — reproducible playbooks & images
